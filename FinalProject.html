My Final Project

Hello everyone! The purpose of this program is to challenge people who love the television shows “Grey’s Anatomy” or “The Vampire Diaries” as much as I do and allow players to test their knowledge on basic trivia of the characters in each series. This can be entertaining for people and be challenging to some who don’t know too much about the show. To play you have to select which show you want to test yourself on and then connect lines to the answers you think are correct. You can check your answers by clicking the "check your answers" button.
